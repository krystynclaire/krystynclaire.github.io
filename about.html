About Us

Emanouil Brothers, Inc. was founded in 1986 to provide landscape services for leading universities and prominent public and private projects. With minimal equipment, a small staff, keen design sense and a hands-on approach, we began creating a company that has since grown to become an industry leader.

Over the past 31 years, we have continued to grow while maintaining our commitment to quality and process improvement. Our customer base has expanded to include a wide variety – from local residences to major estate properties, small commercial buildings to leading universities, and other prominent projects throughout New England.

Today, Emanouil Brothers, Inc. has one of the most extensive landscaping fleets in New England and is positioned to handle a large volume of service contracts within tight timelines with some of the most competitive rates in the region.

We provide our clients with professional consultation, design and installation reflecting the highest standards in horticultural presentation. Through a vast array of specific services, backed by a highly skilled management team and supported by experienced installers, we can guide the most intricate design to fruition.
