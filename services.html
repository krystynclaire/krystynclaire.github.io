Our Services

Emanouil Brothers takes pride in helping our clients to enhance the look and value of their homes. From softscaping, which adds natural beauty, color and texture to a garden, to hardscaping, which constructs features to form focal points and incorporates the landscape in a functional yet elegant way, we will work with our clients to customize a project that will satisfy their needs and complement the space. Should the time ever come to sell the property, getting top dollar will prove much easier with an investment in landscaping.



From Residential to Commerical

Emanouil services both residential and commercial properties. Attractive landscaping can transform or enhance the curb appeal of a commercial property and create an excellent first impression that draws customers and clients to an area. At Emanouil Brothers, our dedicated professionals work with our clients to design a project that is both functional and visually appealing in order to achieve their goals on time and at a competitive price.

An irrigation system is used to create and maintain plants, lawns, gardens, and custom landscapes by ensuring that water is spread regularly and evenly throughout a given area. This can help reduce water consumption and save money by maximizing the efficiency of water usage while maintaining a healthy and beautiful landscape. Emanouil Brothers would be pleased to design and install a customized system to best suit your landscape and your budget. We will also work with you to maintain your system for optimal performance for years to come.
